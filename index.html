Idioms and Metaphors Masterclass

Idioms and metaphors are the true core of standard English communication. Mastering them allows you to look beyond literal dictionary words, understand native films or professional publications effortlessly, and express complex human emotions in a creative way.

Interactive Modalities

This app has two main practice zones to help you remember these expressions quickly without feeling overwhelmed:

  1. Flashcard Studio: Flip the cards to look at the phrase, see a helpful picture, check its meaning, and see how it works in a normal sentence.
  2. Context Practice Lab: Test your skills with three different challenges! You will find multiple-choice questions, definition matching activities, and word puzzles where you can rebuild the sentence step-by-step.
